What Makes Gokurakugai Special

Gokurakugai stands out through its moody urban environment, subtle world-building, and focus on emotional consequences. Rather than relying solely on spectacle, the series emphasizes atmosphere, character dynamics, and the lingering effects of curses on both individuals and the city itself.

Characters

Meet the key characters who operate within the shadows of Gokurakugai.

Main Protagonist

Alma

A quiet and stoic troubleshooter who deals with supernatural cases within Gokurakugai. Alma possesses a calm demeanor and a strong sense of responsibility, often placing himself between danger and others.

Abilities & Skills

Supernatural Detection Spirit Communication Combat Mastery Curse Removal

Personality: Stoic, protective, and quietly compassionate.

Main Protagonist

Tao

Energetic and optimistic, Tao complements Alma with quick thinking and adaptability. While outwardly cheerful, Tao is perceptive and capable when situations turn dangerous.

Abilities & Skills

Enhanced Agility Combat Skills Investigation Quick Thinking

Personality: Energetic, optimistic, and sharp-minded.
